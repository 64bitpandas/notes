MCB C61 is the introductory biological science course intended for non-biology majors. It focuses on the brain mechanisms that drive human behavior.

I took notes for this class in the form of a big Google doc designed as a companion to the course textbook, [Foundational Concepts in Neuroscience: A Brain-Mind Odyssey](https://www.goodreads.com/en/book/show/25620680-foundational-concepts-in-neuroscience), which was written by the professor (David Presti). I intended for it to help me recall the key points and takeaways without needing to re-read the entire textbook next time I wanted to review this content.
